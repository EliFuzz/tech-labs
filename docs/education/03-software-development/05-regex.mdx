**Regex** - is a sequence of characters that define a search pattern.

## Anchors

<table>
  <thead>
    <tr>
      <th>Regex Component</th>
      <th>Meaning</th>
      <th>Example</th>
    </tr>
  </thead>
  <tbody>
    <tr>
      <td><b>`^`</b></td>
      <td>Start of string, or start of line in multi-line pattern</td>
      <td>`^cat`  **cat**, **cats**</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><b>`\A`</b></td>
      <td>Start of string</td>
      <td>`\Adog` **dog**, **dogs**</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><b>`$`</b></td>
      <td>End of string, or end of line in multi-line pattern</td>
      <td>`bird$` **lovebird**, **tweeting bird**, **bird**</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><b>`\Z`</b></td>
      <td>End of string</td>
      <td>`fish\Z` **goldfish**, **jellyfish**, **fish**</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><b>`\b`</b></td>
      <td>Word boundary</td>
      <td>`\bwolf\b`  **The wolf howls**, **Lone wolf**</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><b>`\B`</b></td>
      <td>Not word boundary</td>
      <td>`\Bcat\` **concatenate**, **scatter**</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><b>`\<`</b></td>
      <td>Start of word</td>
      <td>`\<day` **day**, **days are long**</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><b>`\>`</b></td>
      <td>End of word</td>
      <td>`night\>` **goodnight**, **tonight**</td>
    </tr>
  </tbody>
</table>

## Assertions

<table>
  <thead>
    <tr>
      <th>Regex Component</th>
      <th>Meaning</th>
      <th>Example</th>
    </tr>
  </thead>
  <tbody>
    <tr>
      <td><b>`?=`</b></td>
      <td>Lookahead assertion</td>
      <td>`cat(?=s)` **cats**, **catalog**</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><b>`?!`</b></td>
      <td>Negative lookahead</td>
      <td>`cat(?!s)` **cat**, **catch**</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><b>`?<=`</b></td>
      <td>Lookbehind assertion</td>
      <td>`(?<=re)cat` **recat**, **precative**</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><b>`?!=` or `?<!`</b></td>
      <td>Negative lookbehind</td>
      <td>`(?<!re)cat` **cat**, **catch**</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><b>`?>`</b></td>
      <td>Once-only Subexpression</td>
      <td>`(?>foo|fo)bar` **foobar**, **fofoobar**</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><b>`?()`</b></td>
      <td>Condition (if-then)</td>
      <td>`(?(?=d)foo|bar)` **dog**, **food**</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><b>`?()|`</b></td>
      <td>Condition (if-then-else)</td>
      <td>`(?(?=d)foo|bar)` **dog**, **food**</td>
    </tr>
  </tbody>
</table>

## Quantifiers

Add `?` to a quantifier to make it ungreedy.

<table>
  <thead>
    <tr>
      <th>Regex Component</th>
      <th>Meaning</th>
      <th>Example</th>
    </tr>
  </thead>
  <tbody>
    <tr>
      <td><b>`*`</b></td>
      <td>0 or more</td>
      <td>`go*gle` **ggle**, **google**</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><b>`+`</b></td>
      <td>1 or more</td>
      <td>`go+gle` **google**, **gogle**</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><b>`?`</b></td>
      <td>0 or 1</td>
      <td>`colou?r` **color**, **colour**</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><b>`{3}`</b></td>
      <td>Exactly 3</td>
      <td>`go{3}gle` **gooogle**</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><b>`{3,}`</b></td>
      <td>3 or more</td>
      <td>`go{3,}gle` **goooogle**, **goooogle**</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><b>`{3,5}`</b></td>
      <td>3, 4 or 5</td>
      <td>`go{3,5}gle` **goooogle**, **goooooogle**</td>
    </tr>
  </tbody>
</table>

## Escape Sequences

**Escaping** is a way of treating characters which have a special meaning in regular expressions literally, rather than as special characters.

<table>
  <thead>
    <tr>
      <th>Regex Component</th>
      <th>Meaning</th>
      <th>Example</th>
    </tr>
  </thead>
  <tbody>
    <tr>
      <td><b>`\`</b></td>
      <td>Escape following character</td>
      <td>`\$` **The price is $10**</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><b>`\Q`</b></td>
      <td>Begin literal sequence</td>
      <td>`\Q$10\E` **The price is $10**</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><b>`\E`</b></td>
      <td>End literal sequence</td>
      <td>`\Q$10\E` **The price is $10**</td>
    </tr>
  </tbody>
</table>

## Groups and Ranges

  <thead>

      <th>Regex Component</th>

      <th>Example</th>
    </tr>
  </thead>

      <td>Or</td>

    </tr>

      <td>Range</td>
      <td>`[ae]` **a**, **e**</td>

      <td>`[^ae]` **b**, **d**</td>
    </tr>

    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><b>`[A-Z]`</b></td>
      <td>Range of uppercase letters from A to Z</td>
      <td>`[A-Z]+` **ABC**, **HELLO**</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><b>`[0-9]`</b></td>
      <td>Range of digits from 0 to 9</td>
      <td>`[0-9]+` **123**, **45**</td>

## Pattern Modifiers

<table>
  <thead>
    <tr>
      <th>Regex Component</th>
      <th>Meaning</th>
      <th>Example</th>
    </tr>
  </thead>
  <tbody>
    <tr>
      <td><b>`g`</b></td>
      <td>Global match</td>
      <td>`/cat/g` **cat**, **cats**</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><b>`i`</b></td>
      <td>Case-insensitive (PCRE)</td>
      <td>`/cat/i` **cAt**, **Cat**</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><b>`m`</b></td>
      <td>Multiple lines (PCRE)</td>
      <td>`/^cat/m` **cat\nconcatenat**, **cat\nsome text\ncat**</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><b>`s`</b></td>
      <td>Treat string as single line (PCRE)</td>
      <td>`/cat.s/m` **cat\ns**, **cat\nsome text**</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><b>`x`</b></td>
      <td>Allow comments and whitespace in pattern (PCRE)</td>
      <td>`/c a t/x` **concatenate**</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><b>`e`</b></td>
      <td>Evaluate replacement (PCRE)</td>
      <td>`preg_replace('/(\d+)/e', '$1 * 2', '123 456')` **246 912**</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><b>`U`</b></td>
      <td>Ungreedy pattern (PCRE)</td>
      <td>`/a.*b/U` **a test b**</td>
    </tr>
  </tbody>
</table>
